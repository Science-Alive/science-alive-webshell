=======================================================================
                   A LETTER LEFT FOR THE TRAVELLER
=======================================================================
You find this scroll tucked into your pack. The handwriting is spidery
and old, and the ink still smells faintly of woodsmoke.


  Traveller,

  If you are reading this, you have come seeking the treasure that my
  maze is said to guard. Good. Most turn back at the entrance.

  I am the Wizard who built these tunnels, long ago, and I do not let
  just anyone reach what lies at their heart. The maze is a test.
  Hidden through its twisting passages are FIVE FRAGMENTS of a secret
  password. Find all five, speak them at the locked door, and what
  waits beyond is yours to keep.

  A few words before you set out:
    - Every room holds a sign. READ IT before you wander on.
    - Some passages are dead ends. Some are hidden from plain sight.
    - Goblins and a troll have made homes in my maze. What belongs to
      them is not yours to take, and the maze itself will see to that.

  I will be watching how you travel. It has been many years since I
  took on an apprentice, and I am beginning to think it is time...
  but first, find your way through.

                                                            -- W.


-----------------------------------------------------------------------
  Your journey begins in the MAZE folder. To start exploring, try:

      ls            - look around you
      cd MAZE       - step into the maze
      cat sign.txt  - read the sign in a room

  Lost in the dark? Type  cd ~  to return home and begin again.
=======================================================================
