=======================================================================
              THE WIZARD'S FORAGING MAP   --   for my apprentice
=======================================================================
You have proven yourself in my maze, so I am taking you on as my
apprentice. This is a map of that same realm -- the one you just
explored -- showing where each ingredient grows.

  HOW TO FORAGE:
    * Travel with your cursor ONLY -- normal mode, no arrow keys.
    * Use  h j k l  to step,  w  b  to hop words,  f<char>  to dash
      along a line, and  gg  G  0  $  to leap across the map.
    * Land on an ingredient and press  x  a few times to pick it.
    * Gather a whole recipe, then go brew it in the cauldron.
    * Picking does not change the real map -- quit with  :q!  anytime.

  YOUR FIRST RECIPE -- HEALTH POTION:
      mushrooms  .  golden carrot  .  cave salt  .  stir
  (stir happens at the cauldron, not out here in the wild)
